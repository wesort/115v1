Dip

Monday, August 23rd, 2010

Dip, Lisa Gornick’s new film is about an unlikely friendship that grows between pickpocket Jason (played by Robert Sheehan) and Asad (Said Mohamed) a Somalian immigrant aboard a London night bus. It airs Channel 4, Thursday 26 August, 11pm (also on http://www.channel4.com/programmes/coming-up/video/series-5/episode-7/dip).
‘…ratchets up the tension…hugely impressive’
– Jonathan Wright, Guardian
